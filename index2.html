Our story

Founded in 2021, For Happiness is a unique startup that combines the sounds of live music with the rich taste of freshly brewed coffee in a nature-friendly environment. Located near Bogd Mountain on the outskirts of the city, this business project is the joint creation of four young entrepreneurs aiming to make people‚Äôs weekends more peaceful and joyful through music, nature, and coffee. The startup operates with two main services. One team member performs live music (violin), focusing on captivating guests and creating a tranquil atmosphere, while the other members prepare and serve freshly brewed coffee to customers. For Happiness operates regularly on weekends from 7:00 AM to 1:00 PM, providing guests with an experience that blends nature, music, and coffee. In its early days, For Happiness faced several challenges, including financial miscalculations and water shortages, but thanks to the members‚Äô perseverance and creative thinking, the operation steadily improved step by step. Today, For Happiness is a growing startup founded on creativity, resilience, and faith in young people. It strives to create memorable moments filled with music, coffee, and tranquility amidst nature for all its guests.
